Description

Super easy Sausage Gravy and Biscuit recipes that will only take you 15 minutes to prepare and serve!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) can refrigerated jumbo buttermilk biscuits
  • 1 (9.6 ounce) package of ground pork
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 1/2 cups milk
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bake biscuits according to package instructions
  2. Meanwhile, cook sausage in large skillet over medium heat 5-6 minutes or until thoroughly heated, stirring frequently. Stir in flour. Gradually add milk; cooked untilmixture comes to a boil and thickens, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Season to taste with Salt and pepper.
  3. Split biscuits in half. Place two halves on each of plates; top with about 1/3 cup of gravy. Enjoy!
